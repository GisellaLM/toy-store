The world of SpongeBob Squarepants and the critters of Bikini Bottom are mashed together with The Monsters by Kasing Lung! Find many variations of SpongeBob and you can even collect his friends: Patrick, Squidward, Sandy, Mr Krabs and Plankton!

Each figure stands at approximately 3 inches tall and comes mystery packed so you never know what figure you will get until you open the box!

There are 12 different designs to collect and 1 hidden edition chase figure.

Available as a single blind box or a complete set of 12 figures when you purchase a case.
